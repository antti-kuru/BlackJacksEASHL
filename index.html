You just entered to The Black Jacks zone

This is the home of NHL Esports team The Black Jacks, an international NHL Esports team. We compete in the highest level Europe, playing in the ECL Elite league. Additionally, we participate in Germany's National League, the GCL, where we have claimed the championship four times. We've also triumphed in the GCL Cup, winning the prestigious national tournament three times. In the past, we secured the ECL Lite title, the third-highest league in Europe. Our roster is composed of talented players from Germany, Austria, and Finland. All tournaments are organized by SportsGamer.gg
