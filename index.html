You just entered to The Black Jacks zone

This is the home for NHL Esports team The Black Jacks. We are an international team playing in the highest level league in Europe, ECL Elite. We also compete in Germany's National League GCL, which we have won four times. We've lifted the trophy in National cup tournament GCL Cup three times and also back in the day won the third highest level league in Europe ECL Lite title. The team consists of players from Germany, Austria, and Finland. The tournaments are run by SportsGamer.gg
